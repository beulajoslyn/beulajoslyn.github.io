    Triple Data Encryption Standard (DES)
    with One Time Pad

    Describes a way to recieve money more securely. Since, Triple DES uses keys thrice, it is considered to be more secure and is sometimes preferred over the normal DES. The system combines the required input and encrypts with Triple-DES. OTP is a randomly generated number encrypted likewise. Hence Triple-DES was used to secure sender's information and DES was used to secure the OTP. Once the OTP is generated, it is valid only till a specific transaction is completed and later behaves as nonce.

    Bharath Electronics Limited
    Bangalore,India

    This particular internship mainly aims at communication through serial interface. It shows communication between two wireless devices without another device eavesdropping the message. Serial communication is the process of sending data one bit at a time sequentially, over a communication bus or ocmputer bus. We implemeted this using RS-232, PIC-18F25K22 and MAX4800. The information at the PIC is in the form of UART and the output from the PIC is sent to a switch (MAX 4800). The PIC was programmed in Embedded C to decicde what information to pass and to prioritize the proper tranmitter aptly. The output would be shown on the LED display.
